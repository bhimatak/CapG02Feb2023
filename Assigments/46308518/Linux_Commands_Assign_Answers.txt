11.Find out the difference between 
	a.“mv” & “cp”
		mv is used to move / rename files. cp is used to copy files and directories.
	
	b. “rm”, “rmdir”
		rm is used to delete files and directories. rmdir is used to delete empty files and directories 
	c. “mkdir” and  “mkdir -p”
		mkdir is used to make directories and mkdir -p is used to create parent directories as needed.
